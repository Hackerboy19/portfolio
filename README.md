# Hackerboy19 - Professional Portfolio

A modern, LinkedIn-inspired professional portfolio built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Showcase your projects, skills, and professional updates in a beautiful, interactive interface.

## 🌟 Features

✨ **Professional UI** - Clean, modern design with gradient accents  
📱 **Responsive Design** - Perfect on desktop, tablet, and mobile  
💬 **Post Management** - Share updates, projects, and professional insights  
👥 **User Profile** - Display your professional information and achievements  
🎨 **Modern Design** - Gradient backgrounds and smooth animations  
⚡ **Interactive Feed** - Like, comment, and share posts  
💾 **Local Storage** - All data persists in browser storage  
🔍 **Search** - Find posts and projects easily  
📊 **Skills Display** - Showcase your technical skills with progress bars  
🏆 **Achievements** - Display your professional badges and awards
